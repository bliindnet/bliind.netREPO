bliind.net: a networked live performance research studio

we define "networked live performance" as any performance that uses the internet as a means of distribution, interaction, collaboration or as a source of material in real time during performance.

fundamentally we are interested in __new models__ for live performance creation and distribution.

if you would like to keep up to date with this project please fill out your contact details below and you'll get added to our mailing list.
